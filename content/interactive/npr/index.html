The Podcast Payoff
The Audience
Sixty-seven million Americans count them-
selves as podcasts listeners, but who are they?
We sat down with agency buyers and senior
brand marketers to learn more about the
people between the earbuds and the listening
experience that makes this audience-and this
medium-unique.
The Content
Brands are embracing podcasting as a medium
and that means they need content. We speak
with senior brand marketers from LootCrate,
Babbel, and Merriam-Webster about how
they're sourcing the right partners, producing
branded podcasts of their own, and finding the
messages that hit their KPIs.
The Metrics
We close out our discussion of podcasting by
tackling the eternal question for brand mark-
eters: How do you measure success? A new
channel requires new metrics and in a still-
evolving measurement ecosystem, we asked
senior brand marketers and media buyers to
pin down their the numbers they think are
the best indicators of a winning campaign.
